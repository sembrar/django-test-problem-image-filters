Image Filters

With this website, we can,

  1. Create an account.
  2. Then, upload our images.
  3. Scroll through a list of all of our uploaded images.
  4. Select one of our images, apply a filter to it, save the output image along with the filter that was applied.
  5. Scroll through a list of all our filtered images.
